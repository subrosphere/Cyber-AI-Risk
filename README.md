# Cyber-AI-Risk

🚧 Work in Progress

Risk governance doesn't change. Threats evolve. 

This portfolio documents my journey from traditional cybersecurity risk to the rapidly evolving field of AI security, AI risk, responsible AI governance and more. 

## Repository Structure

- **`/credentials`** - Certifications, badges and completed learning paths
- **`/cyber-fundamentals`** - Traditional cybersecurity, risk, controls and governance
- **`/ai-risk-extension`** - AI threat landscape (OWASP, MITRE ATLAS)
- **`/cyber-ai-governance`** - Governance frameworks bridging with both domains
- **`/regulatory-compliance`** - Regulatory context
- **`/case-studies`** - Real-world applications, use-case, incident analysis, lessons learned
- **`/portfolio-artifacts`** - Risk registers, threat models, framework mappings, assessment templates, governance checklists 
- **`/evergreen`** - Advanced topics and emerging research

## About Me

Hi, I'm **Subro**.

In 2023, I made the decision to pivot from a non-technical career into cybersecurity. My interest in the field began while working on third-party vendor risk assessments, where I was introduced to information security, cyber risk, and governance. What started as curiosity quickly became a passion and I haven't looked back since.

Today, I work in the Cyber Risk & Controls function within the financial services industry, focusing on governance and risk management. My interests continue to evolve across cybersecurity, AI security, technology governance, and emerging regulatory frameworks.

This repository documents my continuous learning journey. It captures the concepts I study, the frameworks I explore, and the practical artifacts I create as I deepen my understanding of cyber and AI risk.

My goal is simple: to learn every day, continuously improve and contribute to building secure, resilient, and a trustworthy digital environment.
