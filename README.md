# Cyber-AI-Risk

Risk governance doesn't change. Threats evolve. This portfolio documents my journey from traditional cybersecurity risk to the rapidly evolving field of AI security, AI risk, responsible AI governance and more. 

It captures my learning, artifacts, research notes, frameworks, and hands-on projects as I deepen my understanding of both established cyber risks and emerging AI specific challenges.

The goal is simple: continuously learn, apply, and share knowledge while building the skills required to help organisations manage cyber and AI risks with confidence.
